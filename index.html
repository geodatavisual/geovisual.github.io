Big Trajectory Data Mining

The increasing availability of massive multidimensional data from GPS-enabled devices, Earth observations, and model simulations provide new opportunities for advanced scientific exploration and studies to address critical issues and challenges related to transportation. As more organizations across an expanding range of domains are recognizing the importance of spatial data analysis for more diverse purposes, this website demonstrates some of applications developed based on high performance geospatial computing, GeoAI, GeoVisualization, and cloud computing.
